Privacy Policy for Indus HRMS App

Last updated: March 2026

Indus HRMS App is designed to help employees and HR administrators manage human resource activities including attendance, leave, payroll, and employee records. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

Indus HRMS App collects and processes certain employee-related information necessary for HR management functions:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Employee name, designation, and department
  • Employee ID and contact details
  • Login credentials for authentication

1.2 Attendance and Location Data

  • Check-in and check-out timestamps
  • Location data used solely to verify attendance at designated work sites
  • No location data is stored beyond what is required for attendance verification

1.3 Leave and Payroll Data

  • Leave requests, approvals, and balances
  • Payroll-related information handled within the organization's secure system

All data is used strictly for internal HR operational purposes and is not shared with third parties.

2. Permissions Used

2.1 Location

Used to verify employee presence at designated work locations during check-in. Location is captured only at the time of attendance marking and is not tracked continuously.

2.2 Camera

Used for capturing selfies during attendance check-in for identity verification. Images are not shared externally and are stored securely within the organization's system.

2.3 Storage

Used to allow employees to upload documents such as leave supporting files or profile photos. Files are transmitted securely and stored within the organization's system.

2.4 Notifications

Used to send alerts related to leave approvals, payroll updates, announcements, and other HR activities.

3. Login and Authentication

The app requires employees to log in using credentials provided by the organization. Authentication is handled securely and credentials are not shared with any third parties.

4. Data Sharing

Indus HRMS App does not sell, share, or transfer any employee data to third parties. All data is accessible only to authorized HR personnel and the respective employee within the organization. No external analytics or advertising services are used.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by employees within an organization and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect employee data handled by the app, including secure transmission (HTTPS) and access controls to prevent unauthorized access.

7. Data Retention

Employee data is retained for the duration of employment and as required by applicable labor laws or organizational policies. Upon termination, access is revoked and data is handled in accordance with the organization's data retention policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.

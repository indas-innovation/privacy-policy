Privacy Policy for GPH App

Last updated: Dec 2025

GPH App is designed to monitor and assist riders of GPH during their visits to various schools. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

GPH App does not collect or store any personal information from users.

However, the app uses certain device features solely to support its functionality:

1.1 Location Data

  • Track routes taken by riders
  • Monitor visits to schools
  • Calculate total kilometers traveled

No location data is shared with third parties. Location data is used only in real time and only for operational purposes.

2. Permissions Used

2.1 Location

Used to track school visit routes and total distance traveled.

2.2 Camera

Used internally for app functionality. Images are not uploaded or shared externally.

2.3 Microphone

Used only when required by the app. No audio is recorded or stored without user action.

3. Login and Authentication

The app requires users to log in for identification within the GPH system. Credentials are handled securely and not shared with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

GPH App does not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties. No external analytics or advertising services are used.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data handled by the app.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
